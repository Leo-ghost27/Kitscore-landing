FAQ

Questions creators ask before they join.

Is it free for creators to join?

Yes. Claiming a profile and submitting evidence is free. Creators build their verified reputation by inviting past sponsors to confirm completed campaigns.

What if I don't have much campaign history yet?

You can still claim a profile and start building it — your score reflects what's actually been verified, so it grows as you add confirmed campaigns. There's no penalty for starting small; the alternative is having no evidence-backed profile at all.

How is my score actually calculated?

From mutually confirmed campaigns — you submit evidence a campaign happened, and the sponsor who ran it confirms it. Self-reported claims are always labelled as such and never blended into your verified score.

What's the difference between free and Pro?

Free profiles can link unlimited platforms and submit unlimited evidence — that's free forever. The only thing capped is OAuth-verified platform connections (one, at no cost). Founding Creators get that unlimited free too. See the full plan comparison →
