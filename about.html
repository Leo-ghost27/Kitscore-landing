About Kitscore

Built for the people
making real decisions.

Kitscore is the verified sponsorship reputation platform. We exist to answer one question — "Will this creator actually deliver on a sponsorship?" — with verified proof, not guesswork.

The story

Sponsorship decisions were being made on the wrong signals. Follower counts, reach screenshots, and self-reported media kits were passing as due diligence. Brands were signing creators they'd never verified. Campaigns were underdelivering. And no one had a reliable way to tell in advance.

We built Kitscore to change that. Verified campaign history. Mutual confirmation. Sponsor endorsements. Reliability scores. A platform where every number comes with proof, and every sponsorship decision is backed by data that can't be faked.

We're building the reputation infrastructure for the creator economy. A platform where the creators who deliver consistently get recognised for it — and sponsors can trust the evaluation before they sign.

What we stand for
🔒
Proof over promises
Every score component links back to verifiable evidence. We don't accept self-reported data without corroboration.
🤝
Mutual verification
Campaign history only counts when both the creator and sponsor confirm it happened. No single source of truth.
📊
Transparent methodology
Every weight, every component, every confidence calculation is documented and publicly explained. No black boxes.
