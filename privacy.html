Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This is a draft policy written to match Kitscore's current pre-launch data practices (waitlist signups only). It is not legal advice. Before relying on it publicly — especially once you're collecting creator evidence, accounts, or payments — have a lawyer review it for GDPR, CCPA, and any other regimes that apply to where your users are located.

What we collect

Right now, Kitscore collects only what you submit through the waitlist form on our site: your email address, and optionally whether you're joining as a creator or a sponsor.

As the product launches, this will expand to include account information, evidence you upload to support your Readiness Score (such as screenshots or platform exports), and — once payments are enabled — billing details processed by our payment provider, Stripe. We'll update this policy before any of that data collection goes live.

How we use it

  • To notify you when Kitscore opens and is relevant to you (creator vs. sponsor features)
  • To understand demand and prioritize what we build first

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertisers.

Who we share it with

Your waitlist data is stored with Supabase, our database provider, which processes it on our behalf under its own data processing terms. We don't share your information with any other third party unless required by law.

How long we keep it

We keep waitlist signups until either Kitscore launches and you create an account (at which point your data becomes part of your account), or you ask us to delete it.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you at any time by emailing us at the address below. If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have specific rights under GDPR or the CCPA that we'll honor on request.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not currently use cookies, analytics, or third-party trackers. If that changes, we'll update this policy and add a notice on the site.

Children's privacy

Kitscore is intended for creators and brand sponsors who are 18 or older, and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we collect or use your data, we'll update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you directly.

Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@kitscore.io
